About Us

Many people struggle when it comes to maintaining and establishing habits. Although it takes around 18 days for a task to become a habit, people struggle and find it challenging to stay consistent with healthy habits such as hydration, exercise, and sleep, even if they have the strongest desire or will-power to improve their lifestyles.

As awareness grows about the importance of habits for achieving long term health and personal goals, individuals have difficulty finding motivation or self discipline. They tend to get overwhelmed when dealing with the complications of adding a new habit to their day to day lives, potentially leading to burnout. Stress also impedes habit formation as people who are under pressure can get distracted, and off track. Without any reminders or tracked progress, people may feel disconnected from their goals and lose their motivation.

Obstacles, such as fear of failure, fear of change, or negative mindsets could prevent new habits from sticking in the long run. Understanding these obstacles and how valuable it is to form new habits can help in the creation of successful strategies when introducing them into one's lifestyle.

Creating the Habitz application will give users a way to track progress and eliminate setbacks when integrating new habits into their everyday lives. Some ways this application will improve one's ability to maintain habits is by having progress tabs, activities, and a mood tracker/journal to allow users to customize and ensure they are staying up to date with their habit integration.
